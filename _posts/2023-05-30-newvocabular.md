Began using guillotines disproportionately against Japanese immigrants.

Anti-Japoneseism [ Anchinihongoisme ] - The exstinguishing of the Japanese people from French controlled territories by Ronin, do to its actions against China, Indochina, and South Korea revived by Shinzo Abe and his white washing of World War II. Engaged in unethical science experiments. Also sought to emulate the French in their use of Guillotines, as a more efficient killing method.

Anti-Japanese And Francophone United Front - [ AJAF - Anchinihogoisme Et Furansuisme Primier Linier ] - A band of rogue Poilu and Ronin united in wanting to drive out the French and Japanese military from the US, with radical sections opposing Francophonia and Japanese culture completely. Which later evolves into its own authoritarian idealogy that sought to emulate the worst aspects of both idealogies: Guillotines And Unethical Science Experiments. Enforcers evolved into "Poiluronine".
